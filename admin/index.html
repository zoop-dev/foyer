Sign-up Protection

New accounts created over a detected VPN or proxy are blocked; existing users are unaffected. (Uses proxycheck.io — set a PROXYCHECK_KEY secret for higher limits.)

e.g. once 5 accounts on the same domain (like @zo0p.dev) sign up within 24h, further sign-ups from that domain are blocked.

These domains skip the limit. Leave as-is to exempt the common providers (gmail, yahoo, outlook, icloud, proton…); clear it to rate-limit every domain.

Email Blocklist

Email Allowlist (lockdown)

When Lockdown is on, only these emails (plus admins & owners) can sign in or view the site. Has no effect when Lockdown is off.
