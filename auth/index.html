What is Foyer?

A "foyer" is the entryway before the rooms. Every Foyer site greets visitors with a graceful gate, then opens into a fully custom, page-built experience — all from one codebase, themed and configured per site.

Page builder

Compose pages from stackable sections — hero, collection, gallery, accordion, and more — right in the admin panel.

Gated by design

Google, GitHub, Discord, magic-link, or Foyer Auth. Public, invite-only lockdown, or fully open — your call.

Themed per site

Colors, icons, nav, backgrounds — each site is its own brand on the shared engine.

One control-plane

Live status, announcements, feature flags, and a global changelog across every Foyer site.

The Foyer ecosystem

Foyer is the umbrella. Two pieces work together.

The platform

Foyer

The engine that powers each site — the builder, the gate, the admin, the theming, and the shared control-plane. Build a site once; everything else is config.

The identity

Foyer Auth

One account across every Foyer site. Sign in once and you're recognized everywhere in the ecosystem — passwordless-simple, no per-site setup.
