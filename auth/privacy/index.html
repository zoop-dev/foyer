1. DATA CONTROLLER AND OPERATIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE

1.1 Identity of Data Controller

The Platform is operated, controlled, and administered by Foyer ("Data Controller"), reachable exclusively at zoop@foyer.zo0p.dev for any privacy-related inquiries. You acknowledge that the Data Controller's obligation to respond to any such inquiry is limited, discretionary, and subject to all applicable exemptions, deferrals, and restrictions described herein.

1.2 Technical Infrastructure and Sub-Processors

The Platform is built upon the Foyer framework and operates across the following third-party infrastructure components, each of which independently processes data passing through or generated by your interaction with the Platform:

  • Cloudflare — provides hosting, edge caching, database services (D1), and Workers AI (the administrative AI assistant). All requests, including your IP address and request metadata, are processed by and logged within Cloudflare's global infrastructure. Cloudflare operates under its own privacy terms, which are entirely outside the Data Controller's control.
  • Supabase — serves as the Platform's control plane, processing site status, version data, error telemetry, and heartbeat signals. All control-plane interactions generate records within Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Web3Forms — processes all contact form submissions, including any personal information you voluntarily provide therein. Contact form data is transmitted to and retained by Web3Forms under its own independent terms.
  • proxycheck.io — optionally deployed for proxy and VPN detection at the point of sign-in, if enabled by the Platform administrator. If active, your IP address and connection metadata are submitted to proxycheck.io for classification. You explicitly acknowledge that the Platform's use of these sub-processors is non-negotiable, that you consent to data transmission to each of them as a condition of Platform access, and that the Data Controller bears no liability for the independent data practices of any sub-processor.

2. COMPREHENSIVE DATA COLLECTION DISCLOSURE

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

Upon any interaction with the Platform — including merely loading a page — the following categories of data are automatically, immediately, and continuously collected, logged, and retained without any action required on your part:

  • Page views, navigation paths, entry and exit pages, and complete session interaction sequences.
  • Your IP address, from which your approximate geographic location and country of origin are inferred and recorded.
  • Browser type, version, rendering engine, and user-agent string.
  • Device type, operating system, screen resolution, hardware capabilities, and device fingerprint identifiers.
  • HTTP referrer data indicating the source from which you arrived at the Platform.
  • Precise interaction timestamps, session duration, and time-on-page metrics.
  • Network connection characteristics, including detection of proxy or VPN usage if proxycheck.io is enabled.
  • Service Worker cache interactions and on-device image cache data, retained for approximately seven (7) days per cache cycle.

2.2 Locally Stored Data

The Platform deploys localStorage and session token mechanisms on your device for the following purposes, which you consent to by accessing the Platform:

  • Storage of UI preference flags, version identifiers, and motion/animation settings selected or inferred during your session.
  • An on-device image cache maintained via Service Worker, refreshing approximately every seven (7) days, for performance optimization purposes.
  • A session authentication token maintained for the duration of your authenticated session, used to identify and validate your account state across page loads. You acknowledge that these storage mechanisms are functional and non-negotiable components of the Platform's operation. Blocking or clearing localStorage may impair or entirely prevent Platform functionality, and the Platform bears no liability for any resulting degradation of service.

2.3 Voluntarily Submitted Data

Any information you voluntarily submit to the Platform — including but not limited to contact form submissions, account registration details, support inquiries, or any other user-generated input — is collected, retained, and processed by the Platform and its sub-processors. You warrant that all voluntarily submitted information is accurate, that you have the legal right to submit it, and that its submission does not violate any third-party rights or applicable law.

2.4 Administrative AI Assistant Data

The Platform incorporates an AI-assisted page-building assistant powered by Cloudflare Workers AI, accessible exclusively to Platform administrators. Any content, instructions, queries, or outputs generated through or transmitted to the AI assistant are processed by and may be retained within Cloudflare's AI infrastructure. Administrator interactions with the AI assistant are subject to Cloudflare's independent data processing terms.

3. PURPOSES OF DATA COLLECTION AND LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

3.1 Declared Processing Purposes

The Platform collects, processes, and retains data for the following purposes, each of which you explicitly consent to as a condition of Platform access:

  • Platform Operation and Display: To render, serve, and maintain the Platform's interface and functionality across your device and session.
  • Authentication: To verify and maintain the identity of signed-in visitors across authenticated sessions.
  • Analytics and Traffic Understanding: To analyze aggregate and individual visitor behavior, traffic patterns, geographic distribution, and engagement metrics for the purpose of Platform optimization and administration.
  • Abuse Prevention: To detect, classify, and mitigate spam, automated traffic, malicious actors, and banned users through IP-based bans, proxy/VPN detection, and behavioral analysis.
  • Contact Form Delivery: To transmit contact form submissions to the Platform administrator via Web3Forms infrastructure.
  • AI-Assisted Administration: To facilitate the administrator's use of an AI page-building assistant for Platform content management purposes.

3.2 Legal Basis for Processing

The Platform processes your data on the following legal bases: (i) legitimate interests — operating the Platform, maintaining security, preventing abuse, and analyzing traffic are necessary for the Platform's lawful operation, and these interests are not overridden by your data protection rights in the context of a voluntarily accessed web service; (ii) consent — for any processing beyond what is strictly necessary for Platform operation, including analytics, your use of the Platform constitutes consent, which you may withdraw by contacting zoop@foyer.zo0p.dev, noting that withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing; (iii) legal obligation — where applicable law requires retention or disclosure of data. EU/EEA users may object to processing based on legitimate interests by contacting zoop@foyer.zo0p.dev; denial of basic Platform access is not a consequence of objection to non-essential processing.

4. COOKIES, LOCAL STORAGE, AND TRACKING MECHANISMS

The Platform bears no responsibility for third-party tracking arising from sub-processor interactions.

5. DATA RETENTION

5.1 Retention Schedules

Data collected and generated through Platform interactions is retained according to the following schedules, subject to extension at the Platform's sole discretion:

  • Authenticated session tokens expire and are invalidated after seven (7) days of inactivity or upon explicit sign-out.
  • On-device Service Worker image cache is cleared and refreshed approximately every seven (7) days.
  • Analytics and visitor records, including IP addresses, behavioral data, and interaction logs, are retained for up to twenty-four (24) months from the date of collection, after which they are deleted or anonymized, except where a longer retention period is required by law or an active legal hold.
  • Contact form message data is retained for up to twenty-four (24) months after the related inquiry is resolved, in accordance with Web3Forms' independent retention policies and Foyer's operational email retention practices.
  • Account data and associated records are retained for the duration of your active Account and for an indefinite period following Account termination, to the extent required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, enforcement of the Terms of Service, or Platform security purposes.

5.2 No Guaranteed Deletion

You acknowledge that data deletion requests are subject to the limitations, exemptions, and procedural requirements described in Section 7 of this Disclosure. The Platform makes no guarantee that any data will be deleted within any specific timeframe, and retains the right to preserve data indefinitely where required by legitimate legal, security, or operational interests.
