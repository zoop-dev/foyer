About Foyer

Foyer is a config-driven framework for gated personal sites. The site you came from is built with it. One small config file — name, domain, theme, icons — produces a complete site; everything else is shared, reusable engine.

The idea

Most personal sites are open. Foyer sites start with a foyer: a sign-in entryway. Visitors authenticate — by magic link, Google, GitHub, or Discord — before they reach your content. You get real accounts, sessions, roles, and analytics instead of anonymous traffic.

What's inside

  • Sign-in gate with passwordless (magic-link) & social auth
  • Sessions, account panel, and per-device sign-out
  • A visual block page builder + admin CMS
  • Tutorials, reviews, custom pages, and theming
  • Cloudflare Pages + D1 + Functions — serverless at the edge

Who made it
